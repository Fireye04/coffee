Wild West TTRPG System | Kai's Brain (2021)

D&D 5th edition didn't do the wild west setting justice. So I decided to make something that does. Featuring:

  • D&D 5e combat redesigned
  • Improved cover mechanics
  • Designated confidence systems to mechanically impart the weight of battle
  • Redesigned health and targetting systems
  • Deeper handling of injuries between encounters
  • Defined pierce-soak system for weapons and armor
  • Weapon modification framework
